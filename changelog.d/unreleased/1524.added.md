## English

- **`--color {auto,always,never}` flag (#1524)** — adds a global `--color` CLI flag that explicitly opts in to or out of ANSI color, layered on top of the `NO_COLOR` / `CLICOLOR_FORCE` / `CLICOLOR` env handling added in #1523. `--color=always` keeps colored kind labels through pager pipes such as `cdidx symbols Foo | less -R`, and `--color=never` suppresses ANSI even on a TTY. Precedence: `--color` flag > `CLICOLOR_FORCE` > `NO_COLOR` > `CLICOLOR=0` > TTY auto-detect. Invalid `--color` values are rejected with a usage error and a hint listing the supported values. The flag-stripper respects the `--` query-escape sentinel, so `cdidx search -- --color=auto` still treats `--color=auto` as a literal query argument.
